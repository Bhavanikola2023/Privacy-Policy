If you sign up via LinkedIn, Google, or other providers, we may receive your public profile data (name, email, job title).

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted partners (e.g., email platforms, payment processors) strictly to perform services on our behalf.

3. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Correction: You may request access to—or correction of—your personal data by contacting us (see Section 8).

4. Data Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our security measures are regularly reviewed and enhanced as necessary.

5. International Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country where our service providers operate. By using our services, you consent to such transfers in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us to have it removed.
